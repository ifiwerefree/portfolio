The Thai Cave Rescue:







Synopsis:

The extraordinary Thai cave rescue was an international effort to rescue a youth soccer team who were trapped in the Tham Luang Nang Non cave in Chiang Rai province, Thailand.

The 12 boys and their coach were trapped by floodwaters on June 23, 2018 and were found alive by rescuers on July 2. After a complex and high-risk operation, all 13 members of the team were successfully rescued on July 10 and 11, 2018. .
