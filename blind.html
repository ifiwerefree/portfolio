The Thai Blind Orchestra






Synopsis:

The Thai blind orchestra of Thailand made up of young visually impaired children, is a story of determination and inspiration. These children, who are often marginalized in society, find a sense of belonging and purpose through music and the orchestra. The video showcases their talents and dedication as they overcome the challenges of their blindness to create beautiful music together.
